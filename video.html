Video Work

Gemeinshscaft Home

I produced these three videos on behalf of Bluestone Communications for our client Gemeinschaft Home. They are a local non-profit 501(c)(3) organization who aims to serves individuals who have recently exited incarceration and/or are under court supervision, through residential and non-residential programs.

I filmed and edited these interviews for Gemeinschaft Home to increase actions taken towards Gemeinschaft Home by community members and volunteers and build social media content and engagement.

"Brainstorming"

This was a fun project where the objective was to conceive, shoot, edit and output a video of a short dramatic sequence that tells a story using effective continuity editing.
