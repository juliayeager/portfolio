Design Work

SMAD Day Campaign

SMAD day is an annual networking and professional development event hosted by the School of Media Arts and Design (SMAD) at JMU for alumni, students, and community members.

The project goal was to strategically develop the marketing of SMAD Day ’21 and create a comprehensive campaign to brand the event through print, digital, and social deliverables. All graphics were created through the Adobe Creative Cloud.





Give Solar Brand Guide

GiveSolar a local Harrisonburg social impact organization whose goal is to expand access to clean energy, help others build economic security, and create climate control solutions.This organization works to help facilitate low income households gain access to solar energy by partnering with organizations such as Habitat for Humanities to install solar panels on their homes.

The objective of this project was to create a logo for GiveSolar and develop a comprehensive guide/booklet to define their branding standards.
